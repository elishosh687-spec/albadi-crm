5. Website visitor flow

  1. Open /configurator (direct or via bag-quote-app iframe).
  2. Select color → upload logo → adjust placement.
  3. Configure bag size, quantity, shipping → live ILS quote.
  4. Enter name, email, phone → download PDF.
  5. Backend upserts CRM lead by phone, stores design, sets leadSource=website_configurator, stage INTAKE.

6. CRM operator flow

  1. Sign in at /login (credential: ADMIN_PASSWORD env var).
  2. Leads → open lead → 3D Designer (or Preview → Send 3D).
  3. Customer receives WhatsApp button → completes design → record updates in place.
  4. Saved designs visible under 3D designs on lead overview.
